About The Author: Ashley Arciniega

Ashley grew up in Plainfield, Illinois. Her love for art started at a young age when her cousin, rube, introduced her to Frida Kahlo. She would spend all her free time drawing and painting portraits of animals and her favorite celebrities. Today, her favorite artist include:

  • Ivan Albright
  • Claude Monet
  • Pablo Picasso
  • Salvador Dali
  • Vincent Van Gogh

The realism of Ivan Albright, the beautiful colors and delicate nature of Monet's painting, and the abstraction of Picasso and Dali where some of the biggest forms of inspiration for Ashley.

Now, Ashley just wants to spread her love for art to others and share the most inspiring pieces she has seen.
